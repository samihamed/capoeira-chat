Capoeira Song Bot

Because even the singularity start with a ladainha!

Setup

Start a new chat in the Telegram app.

Search for Capoeira Song Bot or @CapoeiraSongBot

Click on Capoeira Song Bot

A new chat opens automatically

Click on Start on the bottom of the chat

Write literally anything (e.g. "Give me a random song!")
